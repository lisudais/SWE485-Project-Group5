Description:
The dataset was created to analyze different factors that may influence depression among students. 
It includes various variables related to students’ lifestyle and daily conditions such as academic pressure, 
sleep duration, study and work hours, dietary habits, financial stress, and family history of mental illness. 
By examining these factors together, the dataset helps identify how different aspects of students’ lives 
may affect the likelihood of depression.
